Accept online restaurant reservations and table bookings with ease.

== Description ==
Online Restaurant Reservation allows your customers to create online reservations for your restaurant. Your customers can quickly fill up a form and make a reservation. Quickly confirm or cancel the reservation with email notifications.

View [All features](https://wpeverest.com/wordpress-plugins/online-restaurant-reservation/)

View [Demo](http://demo.wpeverest.com/online-restaurant-reservation/)

Get [free support](https://wpeverest.com/support-forum/)

Check [documentation](http://docs.wpeverest.com/docs/online-restaurant-reservation/)

### Features And Options:
* Unlimited booking
* Easy shortcode to display reservation form in any page or post
* Various reservation status like Pending, Confirmed, Check-in, Cancelled.
* Manually confirm or cancel the reservation.
* Min and Max party size allocation
* Email notification on reservation, confirmation, cancellation and more.
* Easily add reservations from admin panel as well.
* Manage Reservation schedule for a week and time interval for each day.
* Manage Reservation exceptions for holidays, special festivals.
* Quick view of reservation details of customer from admin via popup.
* Block reservation on closed schedule and date.
